Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

This is easily one of the quickest meal's to make. With everyday ingredients and a little bit of italian flavour you'll have this meal done in a jiffy.

Directions:

  1. Add spaghetti to a pot and cover with boiling water
    Boil for 15 minutes until tender and then strain in a coriander


  2. Heat up some oil in a pan and add the onions
    Brown the onions and then add the mince
    Brown the mince for about 4 minutes and add your spices


  3. Add the carrots and tomatoes aswell as 1 tin of onion and tomato mix
    Make a small gap in the center and add the puree - give it a good stir
    Finally add your cook in sauce and let the whole thing simmer on low heat
    for another 10 minutes


  4. Serve with grated white cheddar and ENJOY
